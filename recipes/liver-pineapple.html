Liver Pineapple Sausage

There's more to this yellow extravaganza than what meets the eye...

No pineapples were harmed in the making of this dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound liver sausage
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/4 cup mayonnaise, divided
  • 2 tsp unflavored gelatin
  • 2 tbsp cold water
  • stuffed olives, sliced

Recipe Instructions

  1. Mix together sausage, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce and 1/4 cup mayonnaise. Shape around a jelly glass.
  2. Soften gelatin in cold water and dissolve over hot water. Add in remaining 1 cup mayonnaise and chill.
  3. Frost "pineapple" score; stud with sliced, stuffed olives. Top with real pineapple head.
  4. Enjoy!

Serves 6.
