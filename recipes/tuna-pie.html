Tuna and Jell-O Pie

A blast from the past - in a cheese crust!

This gelatinous tuna pie will gave grandma a taste of nostalgia

Ingredients:

  • 1 pkg. (3 oz.) lemon-flavored gelatin
  • 1 1/4 cups boiling water
  • 1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp. vinegar
  • 1/2 cup: chopped celery, pimento-stuff olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • cheese pie shell
  • 2 cans (8 oz)tuna salad

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Dissolve gelatin in boiling water; stir in tomato sauce, vinegar, and seasonings. Chill until slightly thickened.
  2. Fold in celery, olives and onion then pour into cooled cheese pie shell.
  3. Chill thoroughly, then spoon tuna salad on top of pie.
  4. Enjoy!

Serves 6.
