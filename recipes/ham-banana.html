Ham and Banana Hollandaise

A holiday treat, wrapped in ham

This Christmas favorite will leave you the talk of the party!

Ingredients:

  • 6 medium bananas
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 6 thin slices boiled ham (about 1/2 lb)
  • 3 tbsp. mustard
  • 2 envelopes (1 1/4 oz size) hollandaise sauce mix
  • 1/4 cup light cream

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven 400F. Lightly butter 2-quart, shallow baking dish.
  2. Peel bananas; sprinkle each with 1/2 tsp lemon juice to prevent browning.
  3. Spread ham slices with mustard. Wrap each banana in slice of ham. Arrange in single layer in casserole. Bake 10 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, make sauce: in small saucepan, combine sauce mix with 1 cup water, 1 tbsp lemon juice, and cream. Heat, stir to a boil; pour over bananas.
  5. Bake 5 minutes longer, or until slightly golden.
  6. Enjoy!

Serves 6.
